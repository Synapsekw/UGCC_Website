Oil and Gas

Construction projects in the oil & gas industry is a key component of UGCC’s services

UGCC constructs and provides maintenance services for gathering centers, heavy civil foundations, pipe racks, including structural fabrication, erection, and foundations, pipe sleepers, tank farms, pre-engineered buildings, blast-resistant shelters, dikes, ditches, roads, curbs, and parking bays.

UGCC also manages maintenance projects for government-owned oil companies.

Capabilities

In the past, UGCC projects included handling road maintenance services at various sites in Kuwait. The projects also include maintenance works for civil buildings and constructions at the Mina Ahmadi Refinery and an expansion of Heavy Oil MGC, along with upgrading sub-centers' facilities.
