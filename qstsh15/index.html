General Maintenance of Ahmadi Governorate – First Zone, Kuwait

QS-T.SH-15

Project Scope

The scope includes general maintenance and upgrading works: resurfacing and repair of road pavements, drainage system upgrades, rehabilitation of storm-water infrastructure, renewal of road markings and traffic signage, and structural upkeep of interchanges and junctions - all aimed at restoring optimal functionality and ensuring long-term reliability of road and infrastructure networks across the governorate.

Project Details

The QS/T.SH/15 Project’s scope comprises General Maintenance of Ahmadi Governorate – First Zone, Kuwait

  • Earth Works (Excavation & Backfilling)

  • Subgrade Preparation, Aggregate Subbase and Base Course.

  • Asphalt Milling and Repair Works

  • Asphalt Road Works (Prime Coat, Tack Coat, Type 1, Type 2 & Type 3 (Super Pave))

  • Road Marking and Signages

  • Street Furnishing (Kerb Stone, Interlock Tiles, Pavers, etc.)

  • Storm Drainage Networks, Sanitary Drainage Networks, Irrigation Networks, Micro Tunnelling, CCTV works, etc.
