General Civil Works Civil and Tank-C & T at Al-Zour Refinery ZOR-EPC-0059 Project Scope The scope includes large‐scale earthworks, concrete ring-wall foundations for storage tanks (diameters ranging up to 78 m), installation of inserts and leak-detection systems, associated civil infrastructure and tank support works. Works are carried out across the tank C & T plot area, following refinery specifications, with structural concrete, sub‐bases, backfill, civil installations and quality control systems.
