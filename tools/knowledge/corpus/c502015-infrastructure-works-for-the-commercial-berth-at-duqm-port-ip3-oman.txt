Infrastructure Works for the Commercial Berth at DUQM Port (IP3), Oman C50-2015 Project Scope The project scope covers the full civil and infrastructure works for the commercial berth at Duqm Port (IP3), including the construction of roads and access routes, earthworks and pavement layers, installation of potable water, firefighting, electrical and telecommunication networks, storm-water drainage systems, and the execution of manholes, chambers and all required utility structures, culminating in the paving and preparation of operational platforms to support berth activities. Project Owner Special Economic Zone Authority (SEZAD), Oman Consultant Royal Haskoning / Khatib & Alami and Partners Contractor United Gulf Construction Co. W.L.L. Project Schedule Date of Commencement 01-12-2015 Date of Completion 31-12-2020 Project Value 200,600,000 USD (77,100,000 OMR) Civil Infrastructure Building Construction Roads and Bridges Electro Mechanical Project Details Roads and Paving Works Construction of 8 km roads and paving, all internal roads as access to building and parking areas, construction of parking areas, traffic signals, road furniture and road markings, construction of 2 Nos Helipad of area each 2,200m2 and Reefer Gantry 84 units Civil Infrastructure All related earth works Cut/Fill, including Import/Disposal as necessary and pavement structure for both flexible (asphalt) and rigid (concrete and concrete heavy duty) pavements The Infrastructure Works comprised of Potable water network, firefighting network, storm water drainage, sewerage network, pump stations, electrical works, telecommunications, street lighting, yard lighting, spare ducts, fencing and gate works and STP 305 m3/day Building Construction Works The Building Construction Works include building at the commercial berth zone (pre-gate, Gates and inspection zone) is included and ancillary buildings are Pass office building, transaction building, mosque, electrical service building, firefighting water tank, general building, Custom – MOH administration and inspection building, MAF inspection building, MOH clinic, Staff building and ammunition building Statistics 8,911,932 m3 of bulk earth works (Cut/fill/disposal) 1069 Nos pile works – dia 600mm (8m to 12m depth) 53,080 Metric Ton Asphalt Paving Works 145,317 m3 of reinforced (RC) Concrete Works 8,863 metric tons of steel Rebars 4.5 km Potable Network – HDPE Pipe (Dia 225mm) 8.92 km Firefighting Network – HDPE Pipe (Dia 250mm & 315mm) Similar Projects Al Shadadiya Industrial Zone Infrastructure Works, Design, Execution, Completion & Maintenance.
