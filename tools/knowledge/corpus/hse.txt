HSSE Health, Safety, Security and Environment About Credentials HSSE Quality CSR Occupational Health & Safety UGCC achieved international recognition for its health and safety programme by completing certification in Occupational Health & Safety, today maintained to the ISO 45001 standard. Our commitment towards our employees’ well-being and prevention of accidents is summarized by UGCC’s primary safety rule: Zero Accidents. Our personnel continuously inspect changing workplace conditions to address hazards and minimise any potential risks. This policy not only enhances the safety of our work crews, it also reduces injury-related downtime and maintains reduced rates of materials damage. Employee Well-being Prevention of Accidents Zero Accidents Environmental Management UGCC aims to develop and maintain health, safety and environmental guidelines to a high standard, in line with the requirements of ISO 45001 and ISO 14001. In 2012, UGCC first achieved ISO 14001 for its Environmental Management System (EMS) as part of its management system to manage environmental aspects and fulfill environmental compliance obligations. UGCC maintains the ISO 14001 certification today. UGCC recognises that its work operations may have an impact on the environment in a number of ways, and is committed to reduce or eliminate that impact through a sustained effort on maintaining and improving its environmental performance. Environmental Performance UGCC manages its environmental performance through an ISO-certified Environmental Management System based on ISO 14001, applied specifically to the aspects of construction-industry activities, ensuring that its operations are carefully monitored, measured and controlled continuously to manage and improve environmental performance.
