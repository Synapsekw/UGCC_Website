Construction, Completion & Maintenance of Sabah Al Ahmed Residential City PAHW-C-1151-2009-2010 Project Scope The project involved construction and maintenance of Neighborhoods A4 and A5 of the new Sabah Al Ahmed Residential City, consisting of construction of (930) houses with (13) house types, (28) public buildings, (29) electrical transformer substations, site works (walls, fencing, landscaping and irrigation), as well as the complete infrastructure works (roads, utilities, parking lots, etc.). Public buildings were composed of (5) school buildings, (6) mosques and related buildings, Central Marketplace, Health Clinic and Ambulance Building, Library, Social Development Center, Post Office, Social Units, Municipality Building, Police Station, Citizen Services Building, and TSE Ground Reservoir with Control Building.
