Cairo Street Development Project RA-200(R) Project Scope The Cairo Street (Road 35) Development project encompasses comprehensive civil and infrastructural enhancements along one of Kuwait City’s major thoroughfares. Scope of work includes the construction, completion, and maintenance of roads and interchanges, alongside the installation of full utility networks and storm-water drainage systems. The project further involves the implementation of sewerage and other services to ensure long-term operational reliability and improved connectivity. Through meticulous engineering, high-quality materials, and precise execution, the Cairo Street project supports urban mobility, enhances the public realm, and underpins sustainable infrastructure development in Kuwait.
