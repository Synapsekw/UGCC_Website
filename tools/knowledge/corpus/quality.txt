QUALITY Quality Management System About Credentials HSSE Quality CSR Quality Management System UGCC is a multi-disciplinary contracting company that excels in quality control in planning and management of engineering projects throughout the Middle East and internationally. We continuously strive to improve the quality of our performance and, consequently, the service given to our clients. It is UGCC’s strict policy to adhere to the requirements necessary to facilitate the planning and execution of projects, as well as our overall operations, in order to meet the latest ISO 9001 quality standards. Quality Assurance UGCC’s quality assurance programmes are further measured against industry-specific benchmarks for performance and safety, and we pride ourselves on remaining ahead of our regional and international competition on these criteria. ISO 9001 In 2004, UGCC first achieved the ISO 9001:2000 certification for its Quality Management System and currently maintains ISO 9001:2015 certification. ISO 17025 In 2019, UGCC achieved ISO 17025:2017 certification for the competence of the UGCC Central Testing Laboratory for testing and calibration. Competence The ISO 17025 accreditation is an industrial standard for the competence of testing and calibration laboratories, notably for management requirements for operation and effectiveness of the quality management system within the laboratory, and technical requirements related to the competence of staff and calibration of equipment. The UGCC Central Testing Laboratory conforms with the ISO 17025:2017 standard for testing and calibration laboratories, and provides stringent testing services as per international standards—BS, ASTM, and AASHTO—using fully calibrated, state-of-the-art testing instruments with valid calibration certificates.
