CSR Corporate Social Responsibility About Credentials HSSE Quality CSR Ethical Behavior and Professional Conduct An established framework for ethical behavior and professional conduct for United Gulf Construction Company (UGCC). 1. Introduction This CSR Code establishes a framework for corporate social responsibility, ethical behavior and professional conduct for United Gulf Construction Company (UGCC). The Code defines UGCC’s core values of integrity, responsibility, respect, confidentiality, and professionalism, which govern our conduct and guide our actions. As a highly specialised provider of construction and engineering services, we expect all employees, subcontractors, suppliers, and stakeholders to adhere to the Code’s principles and exhibit the highest standards of ethical behavior. The Code emphasizes the importance of making ethical choices, even in difficult situations, and we hold ourselves accountable for upholding the Code’s values. Failure to comply with the Code may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment or contractual relationships. 2. Code of Ethics 2.1 Integrity Act honestly, fairly, and transparently in all business dealings. Avoid any conflicts of interest, or the appearance thereof. Maintain accurate and complete records of all financial transactions. 2.2 Responsibility Comply with all applicable laws and regulations in Kuwait and other jurisdictions where UGCC operates. Ensure safety and quality in construction work, adhering to industry standards and best practices. Protect the environment by minimizing waste, pollution, and other negative impacts. Promote a diverse and inclusive work environment that values each individual’s contributions. 2.3 Respect Treat all individuals with dignity, fairness, and respect, regardless of their nationality, religion, gender, or social status. Respect the customs, traditions, and cultural heritage of the communities in which UGCC operates. 2.4 Confidentiality Protect the confidentiality of company information and that of its clients, partners, and stakeholders. Only disclose confidential information when legally required or authorized to do so. 2.5 Professionalism Maintain and develop professional knowledge and skills to ensure high-quality performance. Uphold the reputation of the construction industry and UGCC by behaving ethically and professionally at all times. 3. Code of Conduct 3.1 Compliance with Laws and Regulations Abide by all applicable laws and regulations, including labor laws, safety regulations, and building codes. Obtain necessary permits and approvals before starting any construction work. 3.2 Health, Safety, and Environment Implement and maintain a comprehensive health, safety, and environment (HSE) management system. Regularly assess and mitigate risks related to HSE. Provide appropriate training, equipment, and resources to ensure a safe working environment. Immediately report and address any accidents, injuries, or unsafe conditions. 3.3 Fair Business Practices Compete fairly and transparently, without engaging in price-fixing, bid-rigging, or other anti-competitive behaviors. Avoid any form of corruption, bribery, or unethical practices in business dealings. Conduct due diligence on suppliers, subcontractors, and partners to ensure their compliance with this Code. 3.4 Labor and Human Rights Comply with all applicable labor laws and international human rights standards. Provide fair wages, benefits, and working conditions for all employees. Prohibit any form of forced labor, child labor, or human trafficking. 3.5 Community Involvement Engage with local communities and stakeholders in a respectful and constructive manner. Contribute to the social and economic development of the communities where UGCC operates. Minimize disruption and inconvenience to local residents during construction activities. 4. Anti-Bribery and Anti-Corruption UGCC is committed to maintaining high ethical standards in all our business activities. By addressing anti-bribery and corruption, third-party relationships, and conflicts of interest within our CSR program, we demonstrate our commitment to ethical conduct, transparency, and accountability. UGCC has zero tolerance for bribery and corruption. We have established policies and procedures to prevent and detect any corrupt or unethical behavior within the company, including guidelines for employee conduct, training programs, and internal controls to ensure compliance with applicable laws and regulations. We also encourage employees to report any incidents of bribery or corruption through a confidential reporting mechanism. 5. Implementation and Enforcement UGCC implements the Code of Conduct with a clear and thorough communication plan to ensure all employees understand the expectations and standards set forth by the company. This communication takes place during on-the-job behaviour, training, and through regular reminders and updates. UGCC provides resources and support to help employees make ethical decisions when faced with difficult situations. The Code of Conduct is enforced with strong commitment from company leadership to create a culture of accountability. This includes ensuring that employees feel comfortable reporting any violations, with clear procedures in place for investigating and addressing these reports. Consistent enforcement is critical to avoid any perceived leniency that could undermine its effectiveness. 6. Periodic Review The Code is a living document; UGCC will review and update it periodically to ensure we are meeting our commitments and continually improving our social and environmental performance. During each periodic review, we assess the effectiveness of the current Code and identify areas where updates or revisions may be necessary. We engage with employees, stakeholders, and external experts to gather feedback and ensure the Code aligns with the values and expectations of the company and its stakeholders. Ultimately, this review strengthens the ethical foundation of the organization and ensures its continued success.
