Al Shadadiya Industrial Zone Infrastructure Works, Design, Execution, Completion & Maintenance. PAI-18P-A-2022 Project Scope The PAI-18P/A/2022 Project’s scope of work includes Design, Execution, Completion & Maintenance of Al-Shadadiya Industrial Zone Infrastructure. The Shadadiya Industrial Zone Infrastructure Works project encompasses the full development of essential services and networks required to support a modern industrial district. The scope includes large-scale earthworks, construction of internal roadways, grading, and preparation of industrial plots. UGCC executed comprehensive utilities installation, including stormwater drainage, sewer networks, potable water systems, firefighting lines, street lighting, and electrical/telecommunication duct banks. The project also includes the construction of culverts, manholes, chambers, and other infrastructure needed to ensure efficient service distribution across the zone. Through precise engineering, high-quality materials, and advanced construction practices, the Shadadiya Industrial Zone is being prepared to accommodate future industrial operations and support Kuwait’s long-term economic development.
