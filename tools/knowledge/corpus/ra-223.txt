Construction, Completion, and Maintenance of 6.5 Ring Road RA-223 Project Scope The scope of work for the 6.5 Ring Road RA/223 project includes full design-build construction and finishing of the roadway, interchanges and supporting structures along the ring road. It covers earthworks, pavement layers, drainage systems, utility conduits, manholes, lighting, signage, and service access roads. Maintenance works are included to ensure long-term operational performance and durability of the constructed assets.
