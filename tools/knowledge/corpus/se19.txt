Construction, Operation and Maintenance of New Mishrif Pump Station, Force Mains and Subhan, South Surra and Fahaheel Interceptors SE-19 Project Scope T he project involved construction, completion, operation and maintenance of the new sewage pumping station at Mishrif, with a design capacity of 340,000m3 per day, at a depth of more than 40m below ground. Additionally, the scope of work included supplying, installation by non-disruptive methods (micro-tunneling), testing and commissioning of the main pressure and gravity pipelines connecting the pumping station to the Ardiya WWTP. Project Owner Ministry of Public Works (MPW) Consultant Kuwait Engineering Office(KEO) / Metcalf & Eddy U.S.A Contractor United Gulf Construction Co. W.L.L. Project Schedule Year of Completion 2007 Project Value 152,000,000 USD (46,100,000 KWD) Civil Infrastructure Electro Mechanical Micro Tunneling Water and Wastewater Project Details The works were comprised of extensive civil, mechanical, electrical, and instrumentation activities, in addition to operation and maintenance of the station for a period of 3 years after commissioning. Scope of Work New Sewage Pumping Station at Mishref as well as Main Pressure and Gravity Network Construction, Operation and Maintenance of New Mishrif Pump Station, Force Mains and Subhan, South Surra and Fahaheel Interceptors.
