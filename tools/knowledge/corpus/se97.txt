Construction, Completion, Commissioning and Maintenance for New TSE Pumping Stations and Reservoirs at DMC, Kabad, and Wafra SE-97 Project Scope The project involved construction of the new treated sanitary effluent pumping stations at the DMC, Wafra, and Kabad locations, each with 5 pump capacity, 2 service reservoirs of 40,000m3 and 170,000m3, 2 surge protection vessels of minimum 340m3 and 600m3 capacities, administration buildings, substations, generator, chlorine and laboratory buildings. In addition to construction, the scope of works included operation and maintenance of the 3 plants for a period of 2 years after commissioning.
