Construction, Completion and Maintenance of College of Basic Education for Girls at Ardiya PA-AFA-77-2004-2005 Project Scope The project involved construction and maintenance of the Girls Campus of the College of Basic Education in the State of Kuwait. The scope of work included academic faculties, communal building, sports facilities, hostel, central plant, multi-story car park, female open air stadium, basement parking, service tunnel, shelters, and associated external works. Additionally, there was extensive surface parking and landscaping of the entire site area of 280,500 m2. Project Owner Public Authority for Applied Education and Training (PAAET) Consultant Pan Arab Consulting Engineers (PACE) Contractor United Gulf Construction Co. W.L.L.
