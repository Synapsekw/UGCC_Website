Construction of Sewer Network for C3B Area – Seeb Catchment, Oman OWWSC/T/2459831 Project Scope The OWWSC/T/2459831/2023/D103/2024 Project’s scope of work includes Construction of Sewer Network for C3B Area – Seeb Catchment, Oman. Project Owner Nama Water Services Oman Water & Wastewater Services Co. Contractor United Gulf Construction Co. W.L.L. Project Schedule Date of Commencement 26-08-2024 Date of Completion 26-08-2027 Project Value 10,300,000 OMR (8,200,000 KWD) Water and Waste Water Micro Tunneling Electro Mechanical Project Details The Scope of work for Contract C3B shall include but not limited to the construction (Supply, Installation & Commissioning) of the following: Gravity sewer network/s ranging inner diameter from 200 mm to 600 mm Gravity sewer manholes (lateral plus trunk) House connection and rider sewers ranging diameter from 100mm to 185 mm Property chambers, House connection chambers, Gravity sewer manholes (for Rider Lines) The contractor shall design and construct the house connections for all plots located in the project area. Single Rising Mains with different diameter C3B Pumping Stations (PS2 & PS4 & PS6) The work shall include the following (but not limited to): Procurement and Construction of pump station, building complete with all civil, electrical, mechanical, plumping, firefighting, HVAC, Instrumentation & control and ICA works. Supply and installation of flow meter chamber, inlet valve chamber complete with all associated works. Provision for power supply from MEDC source till transformer building Construction of roads, fence works. Construction of Roads Crossing (open cut & Trenchless method) and complete with all necessary works.
