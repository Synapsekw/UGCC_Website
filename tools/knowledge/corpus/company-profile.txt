A B O U T
Headquartered in Kuwait with operations 
throughout the Middle East, India, and 
Africa, UGCC serves both government 
and private sectors by providing resources 
and innovative solutions, with an 
emphasis on safety and quality control, 
producing high-quality results. Since 
1975, UGCC has built a reputation as 
one of the most reliable construction 
companies in the region.

UGCC specializes in the design, 
construction and maintenance of major 
highways/motorways, complex bridges, 
viaducts, and tunnels, in addition to 
suburban roads and related 
infrastructure. 
Our capabilities are reinforced by one of 
the largest privately-owned fleets of 
construction plant and equipment in the 
Middle East, which enables rapid 
mobilization of the necessary resources 
for any project. 
For bridges, our successful track-record 
includes the design and construction of 
pre-stressed precast concrete girder, 
segmental, post-tensioned concrete, and 
balanced-cantilever bridges.
For roadways, UGCC leads the way in 
technology and innovation to improve 
and optimize quality of pavements, traffic 
safety, and construction phasing to reduce 
traffic congestion. Having successfully 
implemented the development and 
upgrading of major roadway systems 
under the most challenging conditions, 
our experience encompasses projects of 
varying magnitudes and difficulties. 
As the largest producer of Hot Mix Asphalt 
in the State of Kuwait, UGCC has the 
ability to produce and pave more than 
2,000,000 metric tons of asphalt 
annually, in accordance with the most 
stringent international standards and best 
practices.

performance-based, asphalt material 
characterization based on environmental 
conditions for improved performance by 
controlling rutting, low temperature 
cracking and fatigue cracking.

and water absorption. The test analyses 
determine the suitability of the aggregate 
selection with the Crushing test and 
Abrasion test.
