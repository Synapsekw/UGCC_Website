Project Details

The MEW-6085-2024-2025 Project’s scope comprises Construction of Nine Fresh Water Towers and Annexed Works at Mutlaa Residential City (Site no 3) including the following works:

  • Nine reinforced concrete pre-stressed towers for storage of fresh water (660,000-gallon capacity each).
  • Inlet and Outlet Piping & fittings works
  • Valves and associated fitting works
  • Drainage works, boundary wall, landscaping, plantation, irrigation system
  • Site Internal Asphalt roads, electrical and Lighting
  • Instrumentation and Control Works, HVAC and firefighting works.
  • Electrical and control building electrical and control works are needed for towers, guard rooms and other works.
