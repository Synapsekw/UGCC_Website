Construction, Completion, Furnishing and Maintenance of Package No. (3) for Aircraft's Apron and Taxiways and Service Buildings for the New Passenger Terminal Building (II) at Kuwait International Airport

KP3-CNS-301

Project Scope

The project involved construction and maintenance of highways, bridges, street lighting, storm water drainage, sewer, and other services for the 3rd package of the Subiya Highway. Construction included approximately 30.7 km of 3-lane dual carriageway, construction of 3-Full Clover Leaf interchanges, and construction of multiple utility box culverts for protection of crude oil lines and other Kuwait Oil Company (KOC) infrastructure.
