Project Scope

The project involved construction and maintenance of Neighborhoods A4 and A5 of the new Sabah Al Ahmed Residential City, consisting of construction of (930) houses with (13) house types, (28) public buildings, (29) electrical transformer substations, site works (walls, fencing, landscaping and irrigation), as well as the complete infrastructure works (roads, utilities, parking lots, etc.). Public buildings were composed of (5) school buildings, (6) mosques and related buildings, Central Marketplace, Health Clinic and Ambulance Building, Library, Social Development Center, Post Office, Social Units, Municipality Building, Police Station, Citizen Services Building, and TSE Ground Reservoir with Control Building. Total Build-up Area 610,138 m2 (6,567,470 ft2)

The Sabah Al Ahmad Residential City development is a major strategic urban-housing project by Public Authority for Housing Welfare, designed to provide modern and well-planned residential infrastructure in Kuwait’s new satellite city south of Kuwait City.The project embodies the vision of a livable, sustainable “Garden City” with landscaped open spaces, efficient utilities and quality housing delivery.
