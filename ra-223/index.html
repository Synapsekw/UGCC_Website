Construction, Completion, and Maintenance of 6.5 Ring Road

RA-223

Project Scope

The scope of work for the 6.5 Ring Road RA/223 project includes full design-build construction and finishing of the roadway, interchanges and supporting structures along the ring road. It covers earthworks, pavement layers, drainage systems, utility conduits, manholes, lighting, signage, and service access roads. Maintenance works are included to ensure long-term operational performance and durability of the constructed assets.

Project Details

The RA/223 Project’s scope includes, civil works, road works, water / wastewater systems, and electrical works

Expressways

Construction of dual (3-lanes and 4-Ianes) expressways including parallel collector distributor roads 2 lanes

Overpasses

Construction of seven overpasses, in addition to widening for existing bridges.

Underpasses

Construction of two underpasses

Culverts

Construction of Culvert for KOC pipelines protection

Drainage System

Construction of a storm water drainage network, including pump stations, culverts, pipes, manholes and gullies

Electrical Works

  • Realignment of 132 KV Overhead Cables which cross the 6.5 ring road project between Sulaibiya “AW” S/Stn and W. Jaleeb “AW” S/Stn as per MEW requirements

  • Electricity network of 11 KV

  • Street lighting, Telecommunication, Traffic signs and signals
