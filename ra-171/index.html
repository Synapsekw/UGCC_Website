General Maintenance for King Fahd Bin Abdul Aziz Expressway with Cross Roads

RA-171

Project Scope

The project involved general maintenance and asphalt pavement renewal for highways, and access roads for King Fahd Bin Abdul Aziz Expressway (Highway 40) and King Faisal Ibn Abdul Aziz Motorway (Highway 50), in addition to traffic management, for a period of 5 years each.
