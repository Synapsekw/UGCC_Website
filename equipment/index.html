Overview

In addition to stationary plants and fabrication facilities, UGCC invests considerably in owning and maintaining one of the largest heavy equipment fleets in the Middle Eas

The Equipment Division is managed, operated and maintained by a well-trained, experienced team of engineers and staff, ensuring rapid deployment and continuous support for the company’s projects, both locally and internationally.

The equipment and fleet includes dumpers and trailers, heavy duty cranes, tower cranes, heavy duty dozers, excavators, loaders, graders and a wide range of compaction equipment. In 2015, UGCC undertook a complete upgrade of its
fleet, further increasing capabilities across all business
