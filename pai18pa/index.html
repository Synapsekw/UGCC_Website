Project Scope

The PAI-18P/A/2022 Project’s scope of work includes Design, Execution, Completion & Maintenance of Al-Shadadiya Industrial Zone Infrastructure. The Shadadiya Industrial Zone Infrastructure Works project encompasses the full development of essential services and networks required to support a modern industrial district. The scope includes large-scale earthworks, construction of internal roadways, grading, and preparation of industrial plots.

A cornerstone infrastructure development by UGCC, the Shadadiya Industrial Zone project highlights advanced engineering, large-scale earthworks, and modern utility networks - supporting Kuwait’s economic growth and enabling a fully integrated industrial ecosystem.

UGCC executed comprehensive utilities installation, including stormwater drainage, sewer networks, potable water systems, firefighting lines, street lighting, and electrical/telecommunication duct banks.
The project also includes the construction of culverts, manholes, chambers, and other infrastructure needed to ensure efficient service distribution across the zone.

Through precise engineering, high-quality materials, and advanced construction practices, the Shadadiya Industrial Zone is being prepared to accommodate future industrial operations and support Kuwait’s long-term economic development.
