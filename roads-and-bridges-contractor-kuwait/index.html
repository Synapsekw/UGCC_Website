Roads and Bridges

UGCC’s Roads and Bridges division stands at the core of the nation’s infrastructure development, delivering safe, durable, and high-capacity transport networks that connect people, cities, and industries. Through advanced engineering, meticulous planning, and world-class construction practices, we build the foundations of mobility — from highways and interchanges to complex bridge structures ensuring long-term reliability and sustainable growth for generations to come.

Capabilities

UGCC’s Roads and Bridges division offers end-to-end capabilities — from feasibility studies and design to construction, testing, and long-term maintenance. Our in-house engineering and project management teams ensure that every kilometer built meets international standards of safety, efficiency, and durability.
