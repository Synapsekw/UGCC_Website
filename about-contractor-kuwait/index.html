HISTORY

Since inception, UGCC has not only demonstrated excellent leadership and exemplary professionalism to each and every construction project, but has also built a team of committed professionals that include engineers, managers, accountants and financial executives. UGCC is a contracting company of the highest standards, where professionals of diversified disciplines work together to deliver construction projects of the highest quality.

UGCC Continues To Grow In Size And Importance During The Era Of Increased Public Sector Development In The Middle East, Particularly In Its Main Market Of Kuwait.
During the Iraq occupation of Kuwait in 1990, UGCC had lost almost 90% of its resources.

Soon after liberation, UGCC was among the leading companies who undertook a major role in rebuilding the nation of Kuwait.Better known for involvement with many of the high-profile projects of the post-occupation years, UGCC has made a name for itself in many other aspects of engineering and construction, from infrastructure construction to the construction of major highways and bridges, and implementation of social infrastructure and mass housing.

UGCC is amongst the most sought-after and efficient engineering organizations in the Middle East and represents a tremendous concentration of technical and strategic knowledge in Kuwait and internationally.
