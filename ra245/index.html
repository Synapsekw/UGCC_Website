Construction, Upgrading and Maintenance of Roads and Interchanges for Access to the Future Mutla’ City

RA-245

Project Scope

The scope covers the construction of new carriageways and upgrading of existing highways, earthworks, asphalt paving, interchanges including a 4-level interchange, utilities relocation (street lighting, water lines, high-tension cables), storm-water drainage systems, retaining walls, and long-term maintenance of the completed road network.

Project Details

Section 1- Upgrade of the existing Abdaly Highway

Section 1 is the upgrade of the existing Abdaly Highway from a 3-lane dual carriageway into a 5-lane dual carriageway with a total length of 9.8km. This section starts at the Jahra Road/Abdaly Highway Interchange #36 with the 6th Ring Road and ends at a point north of the proposed interchange #1. Construction of this highway section entails 365,450 cu.m. of excavation, 1,065,500 cu.m. of embankment and 789,340 sq.m. of new asphalt paving.

Section 2- Construction of a new 3-lane dual carriageway

Section 2 is a new 3-lane dual carriageway with a total length of 11.4km extending east-west between interchange #1 and an existing elevated roundabout interchange along Subiya Motorway. Construction of this section involves 4,979,350 cu.m. of excavation, 1,329,300 cu.m. of embankment construction and 292,250 sq.m. of new asphalt paving. Excavated material from this section is to be used for embankment requirements in Section 1.

The two sections will be connected by a new 4-level Interchange, referred to as Interchange #1. Construction of this interchange involves 13,000 LM of ramps, of which 7,600 LM will be deck-on-pier structures.

The Main Works under this Contract RA/245 (approximately)

  • At-grade Road

    • Upgrade of about 9.80km portion of existing Abdaly Road into 5-lane dual carriageway road with associated auxiliary lanes, C-D roads and service roads.

    • Construction of about 11.40 km of new 3-lane dual carriageway road with associated auxiliary lanes, C-D roads and service roads.

  • Interchanges and Elevated Bridges

    • Construction of one (1) 4-level Interchange with about 7600 LM of deck-on-pier structures.

    • Modification of approach to one (1) elevated roundabout interchange.

    • Box culvert underpass structure at Km3+200 along Abdaly Road.

  • Reinforced concrete retaining walls.

  • Mechanically Stabilized Earth (MSE) walls at bridge and underpass approaches.

  • Stormwater drainage for roadways including concrete-lined ditches, curb inlets, drop inlets and connecting pipes.

  • Reinforced concrete box culverts for cross drainage.

  • Pedestrian Bridge.

  • Utilities

Along these roads, existing utilities needs to be removed, reinstalled, protected or relocated. These are:

  • Street lighting

  • water lines

  • Construction of utility box culverts

  • Civil works for telephone (PTT) services

  • High tension cables over heads

  • High tension cables under ground

  • Medium tension cables over heads

  • Medium tension cables under ground

  • Low tension cables.

There is a need to relocate these lines to meet the required clearance:

  • Road signs and pavement markings, ITS devices and associated works

  • 2 lay-bys

  • Maintenance and protection of traffic during construction (temporary detours)

  • Environmental protection measures during construction

  • Demolition of existing roads

  • Other works related to local roads and junctions
