United Gulf Construction Company handles various types of electro-mechanical works

Capabilities

UGCC’s electro-mechanical works include projects such as sewage pumping stations, treatment plants, street lighting for roads and motorways, fire fighting systems and oil industry piping works. Past projects include the supply, erection, commissioning and maintenance of street lighting at Dasman, Shamiya and Subiya. The projects also include construction of a new 30” HP gas transmission line in northern Kuwait and the construction, commissioning and maintenance of pumping stations in DMC, Kabad and Wafra.
